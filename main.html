About

KYA is a Discord-Nuke-Client, which allows you to execute multiple commands in an own client through your preferred bot, provided you have its token. This project was launched in May 2021 by two German Developers.

History

  • Wednesday, May 12th 2021

    The first launch

    Today we, Yaaqez & Luca decided spontaneous to begin a new, unique and game changing project in the area of Discord-Nuking. So, we avidly started to set up a test-bot and ran the first lines of code.

  • Saturday, May 15th 2021

    From Bot to Client

    After careful thinking we came to the decision to develop a Nuke-Client rather than a Nuke-Bot, because a client (app) allows us to design the bot more unnoticeable and therefore much stronger. In addition to that, we named our project "KYA".

  • Wednesday, May 19th 2021

    A bit of variety

    Since we gave our project a name, we coded a lot in the last days to put KYA into effect. So now, it's time to have a bit of variety by designing our logo.

  • Saturday, May 22th 2021

    Some progress

    Besides designing the images for our socials, releasing KYA's YouTube-Channel and beginning to set up a discord server, we finally added all spy-commands and made the console, in which you execute the bot-commands, more colorful.

  • Tuesday, May 25th 2021

    The final touch

    Since we had everything ready so far, we looked over everything again and embellished the code and the client to have a better overview and to be satisfied. After that we released the client/bot for you all so you can download it.

  • Friday, June 2nd 2023

    Website - html/css

    After a while of stagnation, I (Luca) decided to create a website for the client. I used the programming language html/css and had a lot of experience with it. After I finished the website, I knew html/css even better and I had a lot of fun programming this website. First I created the whole "WEB" page, after that the "SUPPORT" page and last but not least the "DOWNLOAD" page.
